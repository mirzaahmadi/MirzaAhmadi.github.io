CS110110 was my first introduction to web development and computer science. The course focused on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which initially presented a challenge in the lecture sessions. However, the bi-weekly practical sessions provided a platform to apply the concepts and solidify my understanding. Creating a personal website was an effective way to consolidate the knowledge gained from the lectures and showcase my skills.
While I enjoyed the practical sessions, I encountered several difficulties, particularly in creating a functional form for my website. The complexity of JavaScript made it challenging, and I had to rely on external resources and in-class examples to overcome the hurdle.
Although I learned a lot during the course, I acknowledge that I could have invested more effort in the practical sessions. While I spent considerable time refining the usability and aesthetic aspects of my website, I could have included more features to enhance its professionalism.
Overall, I am pleased with my website and feel confident in my web development skills. I look forward to advancing my knowledge through future projects.
